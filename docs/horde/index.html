Click the castle to train militia. Click a wall slot to place / upgrade an archer tower. Click the wall to upgrade it.

How to play

  • Click the castle, wall, or a tower slot to build / upgrade.
  • Press Play (top-left) to start the next wave.
  • Orcs march from the top. If the wall breaks, towers stop firing and orcs run for the castle.
  • Militia stand around the castle and engage anything that gets past the wall.
  • Castle at 0 HP = run over. Gold and upgrades reset; press Play to try again.
