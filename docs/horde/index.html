Survive all 5 waves to clear Phase 1.

How to play

  • Orcs march from the top. Your wall blocks them; towers shoot them.
  • If the wall breaks, towers stop firing and orcs run for the castle.
  • Militia stand around the castle. They engage anything that gets past the wall.
  • Castle at 0 HP = game over. Gold and upgrades reset.
